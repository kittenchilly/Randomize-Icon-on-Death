# Randomize Icon on Death

Everytime you die or restart a level, it randomizes your icons, colors, effects, etc. based on what is enabled in the mod settings. Only chooses ones you have unlocked.
For people that really can't decide what to use.

If you find any bugs or have any ideas, please post them on [GitHub](https://github.com/kittenchilly/Randomize-Icon-on-Death/issues) or message me on Discord @kittenchilly (I am in the Geode server)!
